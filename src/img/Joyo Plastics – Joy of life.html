About JOYO


Joyo began in the year 1984, with just one injection – moulding machine and one single mould. His emphasis on quality and innovation quickly won Joyo the trust of thousands of customers.

Today we have 100 injection moulding machines with ranging from 50 tons to 450 tons. We now have 3 facilities that are equipped with cutting-edge technology and machinery with international standards audited by Walmart and Disney. Our extensive line manufacturing brings to the customer a wide range of products like Bathroom Accessories, Kitchenware, Utility products, Containers etc.
